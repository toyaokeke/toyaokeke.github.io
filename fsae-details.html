Designing a CVT

Project Information

  • Category: Mechanical Engineering Design
  • Client: UAlberta Formula SAE
  • Number of Teammates: 4
  • Project URL: Click either CVT Full Report, CVT Poster, or CVT Presentation.

The goal was to design a CVT that could maintain peak acceleration during vehicle operation. The CVT had to be lightweight, inexpensive and mount to the existing vehicle transmission. Our design was showcased later and presented to industry representatives, students and faculty. If you would also like to see other proposed designs you can view the phase 2 report here.
