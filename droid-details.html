Building a Droid to Navigate an Obstacle Course

Project Information

  • Category: Mechanical Engineering Design
  • Number of Teammates: 4
  • Project URL: Click here to view the report.

This was the first project where we got to manufacture our design and have it compete against other designs. The event was broadcasted on CTV News and open to the school to attend. The challenge was to build a droid that could navigate one of three tracks (each track varies in difficulty) given limited materials. The droid had to capture targets using a mounted GoPro camera within a set time constraint to collect points. Points were given for completing a track and collecting targets.
