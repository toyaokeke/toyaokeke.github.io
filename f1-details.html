F1 Race Car Design

Project Information

  • Category: Numerical Simulation
  • Number of Teammates: 5
  • Project URL: F1 Race Car Report

Each team member was responsible for a different aspect of the design. The goal was to optimize the aerodynamics, structural integrity and heat transfer of the vehicle. My responsibility was to design the whole car, add the components made from my teammates and optimize it for aerodynamic performance. All simulations and parts were made using SOLIDWORKS.
